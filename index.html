Some rules to keep in Mind

  • The Game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. Each result get added to his round score
  • But, if the player rolls a 1, then the player looses all his scored score and his turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his round score gets added to his total score and after that he losoes his turn
  • The first player to reach 100 score or defined score wins the game
  • Player looses all his score if he sorres 6 twice in a row
